@ Biography

I'm Yuzhe Zhang, currently a third-year Master's student at Computational Imaging and Sensing Lab @CIS Lab, Beijing Institute of Technology, advised by Prof. Liheng Bian and Jun Zhang. Previously, I received my B.Eng. degree from the Zhengzhou University.

I'm broadly interested in the fields of computational photography and computer vision. My current research focuses on multi-dimensional imaging, image restoration, and image generation.
